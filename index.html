About Us

Staffs Industrial Removals provides specialist industrial services across the UK, handling everything from single‑lift jobs to full engineering‑led projects.

We specialise in machinery moving, factory relocations, workshop clearances, and complex heavy lifting operations across multiple sectors. Unlike generic lift and shift services, our work is highly technical — combining precision lifting with engineering expertise. As an engineer‑led, family‑run business with decades of hands‑on experience, we also undertake fabrication work, bespoke lifting solutions, machine rebuilds, and on‑site problem solving.

Our skilled team is trained to handle all types of equipment and machinery safely and efficiently, ensuring your project is completed to the highest standard from start to finish.

❮ ❯

Accreditation & Compliance

We are fully trained and licensed to operate a wide range of lifting equipment and machinery, ensuring all work is carried out safely, efficiently, and to the highest professional standards. We can also provide full lift plans, act as Appointed Persons, and supervise all lifting operations from planning through to completion.

Staffs Industrial Removals is a member of the RHA and operates in line with recognised industry standards and is registered with the SafeContractor scheme.
